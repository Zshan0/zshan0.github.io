Polar Decomposition
Every matrix can be expressed as a product of an orthonormal and a positive semidefinite matrix.
Let AAA﻿ be a m×nm \times nm×n﻿ matrix where m≥nm \geq nm≥n﻿ then:
A=QPQ∈Cm×nP∈Cn×nA = QP \\ Q \in \mathbb{C}^{m \times n} \\ P \in \mathbb{C}^{n \times n} \\A=QPQ∈Cm×nP∈Cn×n
Here QQQ﻿ is an orthonormal matrix and PPP﻿ is a positive semi-definite.
