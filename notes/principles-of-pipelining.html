Principles of pipelining
The key idea of pipelining is splitting the whole process into discrete pieces which can be performed independently. this increases the overall efficiency of the system since more jobs are completed than when they are done sequentially, but since 1 process has to go through all the pieces it increases the time it takes to complete 1 job, in other words its latency increases.
