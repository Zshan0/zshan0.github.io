Measurements [CURRENT]
A system can't be studied fully until its interactions with an external entity are explained. For our systems, these interactions are referred to as measurements.
Dynamics
A change in the system is brought and explained with the concept of an operator
An operator when applied to a system results in a new system. An example being the case where the filtered Sx+S_{x^+}Sx+​﻿ is passed through the z filter.
